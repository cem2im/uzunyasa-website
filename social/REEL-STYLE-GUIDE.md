# Reel Style Guide — UzunYaşa Instagram

## Reference: Blue Zones Reel (Feb 2026)

### Format
- **Aspect:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920)
- **Duration:** 15-30 seconds
- **Music:** Calm ambient (e.g., "Apesaw - Peace Of Mind")

### Scene Structure
1. **Hook/Title (3-5s):** Cinematic background + large bold text (topic title)
2. **Content Slides (3-5s each):** Clean numbered list on atmospheric background
3. **CTA (3-5s):** Brand + website reference

### Visual Style
- **Backgrounds:** Cinematic nature (ocean sunset, dark forest, mountains, dawn)
- **Text:** Large, white, bold, readable on mobile
- **Font feel:** Clean sans-serif or elegant serif
- **Colors:** White text, sometimes orange (#E8963E) accent
- **Logo:** Small corner watermark (not center)
- **Mood:** Calm, trustworthy, educational — NOT flashy or salesy

### Content Types
1. **Numbered List:** "X Şaşırtıcı Gerçek" / "X Sırrı" / "X Kural"
2. **Fact Reveal:** Single shocking stat + explanation
3. **Comparison:** "X vs Y — Hangisi Daha İyi?"
4. **Myth Buster:** "Bu Doğru mu?" + reveal

### What NOT to Do
- No human faces
- No sales language
- No TikTok watermarks
- No cluttered text (max 3 lines per slide)
- No fast transitions (smooth fades preferred)
